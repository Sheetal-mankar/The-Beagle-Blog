The Beagle Blog

Beagle, small hound dog breed popular as both a pet and a hunter. It looks like a small foxhound and has large brown eyes, hanging ears, and a short coat, usually a combination of black, tan, and white. TheBeagle is a solidly built dog, heavy for its height. It generally excels as a rabbit hunter and is typically an alert, affectionate dog. There are two sizes recognized in the breed: Beagles standing 13 inches (33 cm) or less at the withers and weighing under 20 pounds (9 kg) and those standing more than 13 inches but not exceeding 15 inches (38 cm) at the withers and weighing 20 to 30 pounds (9 to 14 kg).
